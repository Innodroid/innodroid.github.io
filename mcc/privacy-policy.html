Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2025

Memory Care Clock is a mobile app designed to support seniors with dementia, Alzheimer's, or other cognitive challenges by providing a simple, clear daily schedule and helpful reminders. This policy explains how we handle your information. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to keeping your data safe.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (such as name, email, address, etc.)
  • Health or medical information
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar entries
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

There is no login or account required to use the app, and no data is sent to external servers.

Local Data Storage Only

All schedules, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device. This includes information entered by a caregiver, such as custom event names or selected voices.

The app does not use iCloud or any remote storage. If the app is deleted, all locally stored data will be removed.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, tracking tools, advertising networks, or social media plugins.

In-App Purchases

Memory Care Clock offers optional in-app purchases that allow users to support the continued development of the app. These purchases do not unlock features or affect functionality in any way. The core features of the app are free for all users.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for unsupervised use by children. The caregiver settings area can be protected by a PIN to ensure that only authorized individuals manage the app's configuration.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. If we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
